About LungPredict

LungPredict is an AI-powered solution designed to assist in early detection of lung diseases. Leveraging advanced convolutional neural networks (CNN), it can analyze uploaded chest X-ray images to predict potential lung conditions. This project aims to support healthcare professionals and individuals in making informed decisions about lung health by offering fast and accurate predictions.

Additionally, the integrated chatbot can answer questions related to lung diseases, helping users understand symptoms, prevention, and treatment options. By combining image-based prediction and conversational AI, LungPredict strives to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare for better outcomes.
